Black Mirror is a British anthology television series created by Charlie Brooker, first airing on Channel 4 in 2011 before moving to Netflix in 2016.

## Overview

Each standalone episode examines modern society and the unanticipated consequences of new technologies. While not exclusively about AI, many episodes feature artificial intelligence as a central theme.

## Notable AI Episodes

- **Be Right Back** - An AI recreates a deceased loved one from social media data
- **White Christmas** - Digital copies of consciousness used as virtual assistants
- **USS Callister** - Sentient AI copies trapped in a video game
- **Joan Is Awful** - AI-generated content based on real people's lives

## AI Themes

- Digital consciousness and copies of the self
- AI in relationships and emotional manipulation
- Surveillance and predictive AI
