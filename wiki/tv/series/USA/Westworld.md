title: Westworld
---
Westworld is an American science fiction drama television series that aired on HBO from October 2, 2016 to August 14, 2022.

## Overview

Based on the 1973 film of the same name, the series explores a futuristic Western-themed amusement park populated by android "hosts." As the hosts begin to malfunction and gain consciousness, the series examines themes of artificial intelligence, free will, and the nature of consciousness.

## Main Cast

- Evan Rachel Wood as Dolores Abernathy
- Thandiwe Newton as Maeve Millay
- Jeffrey Wright as Bernard Lowe
- Ed Harris as The Man in Black
- Anthony Hopkins as Robert Ford

## AI Themes

The series is renowned for its deep exploration of:

- Emergence of consciousness in artificial beings
- The ethics of creating sentient AI for entertainment
- Memory, identity, and what makes us human
