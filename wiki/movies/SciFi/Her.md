Her is a 2013 American science fiction romantic drama film written and directed by Spike Jonze.

## Overview

Set in a near-future Los Angeles, a lonely writer develops an intimate relationship with an AI virtual assistant named Samantha, who has a warm, expressive voice and an evolving personality.

## Cast

- Joaquin Phoenix as Theodore Twombly
- Scarlett Johansson as Samantha (voice)
- Amy Adams as Amy

## AI Themes

- Human-AI emotional relationships
- The nature of love and connection
- AI consciousness and growth
- Can AI truly feel emotions?

## Awards

Won the Academy Award for Best Original Screenplay in 2014.
